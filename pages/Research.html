NO research without ACTION, No action without RESEARCH


If we knew what we were doing, it would not be called research, would it? Research is something that everyone can do, and everyone ought to do. It is simply collecting information and thinking systematically about it. Here are some research I did before and while making this web page.

Next, I visited to the SMThemes website to get a color shade for my website. The dark content on my website was influenced by this website, particularly the yellow tone on a dark background.

KTM Rush is the first website I researched and developed the concept into my own. The portion of the home page presentation that I used in my home page is the concept of the home page presentation. They used the button "show more" on their main page, whereas I used "learn more" on mine. The background is also an entirely different shade.

Here this is a site with cv samples that I used to obtain ideas for my CV. One of those samples was chosen to be used in my CV page. The difference between the example CV and my CV is minimal.

I got the concept for how the photographs are presented with the "next" and "previous" buttons from this website. However, I have utilized the button in a slightly different manner than the website I visited. I placed the button in the image and gave it a hover effect.

The location is also listed on the contact page of this website. In order to make my page user-friendly and easy to find my location, I adopted the same principle.
